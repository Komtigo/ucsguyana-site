Who We Are

Untouchable Computer Service began as an idea in 2012, conceived by our CEO, Roydel D. Joseph. What started as a one-man operation in 2013 has since grown into a trusted business serving both the local community and beyond.

In June 2014, we formalized our commitment to excellence by registering with the Guyana High Court Registrar. Our mission is simple: to deliver exceptional service on time, every time.

With extensive experience in IT services, architectural design, web development, multimedia editing, digital marketing, and more, we offer a one-stop solution for all your technological and creative needs.
